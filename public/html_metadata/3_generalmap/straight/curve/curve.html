curve

noun [ C ]

UK /kɜːv/ US /kɝːv/

1. a line that bends continuously and has no straight parts

 

2. the curved shape in which a ball moves when it is hit or kicked in a particular way

 

 

curve

verb [ I ]

UK /kənˈdɪʃ.ən/ US /kənˈdɪʃ.ən/

1. to form a curve, or move in the shape of a curve
