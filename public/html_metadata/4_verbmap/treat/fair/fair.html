fair

adjective

UK /fe…ôr/ US /fer/

1. treating someone in a way that is right or reasonable, or treating a group of people equally and not allowing personal opinions to influence your judgment

 

2. If something is fair, it is reasonable and is what you expect or deserve

 

3. If a game or competition is fair, it is done according to the rules

 

4. quite large

 

5. neither very good nor very bad

 

6. (of an idea, guess, or chance) good, but not excellent

 

7. (of weather) pleasant and dry

 

8. (of a woman) beautiful
