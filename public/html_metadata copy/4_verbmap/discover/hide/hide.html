hide

verb

UK /haɪd/ US /haɪd/

1. to put something or someone in a place where that thing or person cannot be seen or found, or to put yourself somewhere where you cannot be seen or found.

 

2. to prevent something from being seen

 

3. to not show an emotion

 

4. If you hide information from someone, you do not allow that person to know it

 

 

hide

noun

UK /haɪd/ US /haɪd/

1. the strong, thick skin of an animal, used for making leather

 

2. a place where people can watch wild animals or birds without being noticed by them
