understand

verb

UK /ˌʌn.dəˈstænd/ US /ˌʌn.dɚˈstænd/

1. to know the meaning of something that someone says

 

2. to know why or how something happens or works

 

3. to know how someone feels or why someone behaves in a particular way

 

4. to know or realize something because you have been told it

 

5. used when making certain that someone knows what you mean and will do as you want:
