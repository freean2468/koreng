degree

noun

UK /dɪˈɡriː/ US /dɪˈɡriː/

1. (an) amount or level of something

 

2. any of various units of measurement, especially of temperature or angles, usually shown by the symbol ° written after a number

 

3. a course of study at a college or university, or the qualification given to a student after he or she has completed his or her studies
