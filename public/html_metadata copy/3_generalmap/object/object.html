object

noun

UK /ˈɒb.dʒɪkt/ US /ˈɑːb.dʒɪkt/

1. a thing that you can see or touch but that is not usually a living animal, plant, or person

 

2. a noun or noun phrase that is affected by the action of a verb or that follows a preposition

 

3. a reason for doing something, or the result you wish to achieve by doing it

 

4. someone or something that causes particular feelings in or actions by others
